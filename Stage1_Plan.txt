SDR Daily Activities

5 structured steps that convert research into signups

1  Find New Startups

   Search LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Product Hunt, and GitHub for early-stage companies that match the ICP.

2  Identify Decision Makers

   Locate the Founder or CTO -- the person who can approve a trial account.

3  Send Outreach Messages

   Send personalized, concise messages referencing the startup's product or known pain point.

4  Track All Responses

   Log every reply in the prospect database -- never let a lead fall through the cracks.

5  Encourage Platform Signups

   Guide interested prospects to create an account and run their first security scan.

5 Team Operating Rules

Non-negotiable standards every team member follows

 1 Every Prospect Must Be Recorded
              If a startup is contacted, it must appear in the database -- no exceptions, no side lists.

 2 No Duplicate Outreach
              Two SDRs must never contact the same startup. Check the database before every outreach.

 3 All Interactions Logged
              Every conversation and reply must be recorded in the Notes column the same day it happens.

 4 Daily Database Update
              All prospect statuses must be updated before end of day -- no deferrals to the next morning.

 5 Follow-Up Discipline
              If a prospect doesn't respond, follow up 3 times over 14 days before archiving the lead.
